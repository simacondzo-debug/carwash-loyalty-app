Did You Know

Free CBD Drop-Off & Pick-Up

Drop your car off at THE CARWASH @ SHELL, and once your wash is complete, we can bring it back to your requested CBD location for free.

This convenience service is available within the CBD for customers who need their vehicle returned to an office, workplace, or nearby CBD location after the wash is done.

After delivery, please assist by dropping our staff member back at THE CARWASH @ SHELL.
